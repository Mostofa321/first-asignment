Our Donate Plan

We spend donor grants in different sectors according to the needs of different
classes of people, some of which are given below.

Food for a family

Shelter for a family

Education for a child

Another of our charitable sectors is providing education to children deprived of education due to poverty and striving for their bright future.

About Us

We are a non-profit organization. Our goal is to help the poorest sections of the society by taking relief from the rich people of the society and to provide food, clothing and shelter to the flood affected people and the poor people. And we consider it our responsibility to provide quality education to the children deprived of education due to family poverty and to strive for their bright future.
